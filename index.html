Overview

Team

 
We are a small team of enthusiasts, engineers, enterpreneurs and most of all human-geeks. We love to simplify the usability of complex technologies and move them closer to adoption one step at a time. We pay attention to simplicity and we execute with surgical precision. We value ease-of-use, application stability, privacy, and security. We work, and get paid at anytime of the day and night and from anywhere in the World. We see no temporal and spacial borders and we think there is something deeply broken with today's small remittance systems. That is why borderless email-like transfer of value is close to our hearts.
 

Please feel free to contact us should you be interested in participating in development of our vision.

Stellar Fox founded.

First working Federation Service prototype.

We have released a Testnet version of our onboarding platform with integrated Federation Service. Users can register their custom payment address, which is discoverable across different wallets and payment providers. (deprecated - build into platform)

Federation Alpha

Remittance Platform.

Send money to any address or a contact in your address book within few seconds. Create escrow accounts with any of your contacts or use us as a 3rd party escrow mediator. Free.

Onboarding Payment Gateways

Integrate available official payment gateways like Stripe or PayPal to provide alternative ways of depositing funds to user accounts.

Anchor Integration

Establish Stellar Anchors in Euro-Zone, Australia and Thailand. Issue SFX-EUR, SFX-AUD and SFX-THB tokens for Thailand directed remittances originating in European Union and Australia. Add curated anchor partnerships to inject liquidity and allow the users to get best possible exchange rates.

Things to come

Here are few things from our backlog.

1 PIN

Sign transactions with your PIN.

We want to find a sweet spot between responsibility, security and ease of use. Our goal is for you to be always in full control of your account. Our platform and wallet is already cross-compatible with Ledger Nano S, a world class cryptocurrency hardware wallet.

Transluminal Shift

Maximum 10 seconds remittance time.

We will strive to design a system that completes your transactions within 10 seconds or less.

Fully Automated Recurring Payments

Fraction of a Cent transaction fees.

The technology we are using is state of the art. Custom built in order to solve current cross border payment problems.

Pay to contact

Email-like payments.

No need to remember any account numbers, bank routing numbers, IBANs, SWIFTs, BICs and whatever else. Just find a contact in your address book. Send money. That's all.
